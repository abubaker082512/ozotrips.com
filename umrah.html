Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required to process an Umrah visa?

You need a Pakistani passport valid for at least 6 months, a computerized CNIC/NICOP, passport-sized photographs with white backgrounds, and vaccination certificates (Meningitis/Polio).

Can packages be customized for family groups?

Yes! We specialize in custom tours. We can arrange triple/quad sharing rooms, private GMC Yukon vehicles, and wheelchair-accessible services for elderly parents.

Are flights direct or connecting?

Premium and VIP packages secure direct flights via Saudi Arabian Airlines or PIA. Economy packages may utilize connections via Gulf airlines (e.g. FlyNas, Emirates, Gulf Air) to optimize budget.
