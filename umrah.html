🕌 Sacred Sightseeing

Explore Holy Ziyarat Attractions

Guided excursions to the most important historical and spiritual sites in Makkah & Madinah are included in all packages.

Makkah

Masjid al-Haram & Holy Kaaba

The largest and holiest mosque in the world, surrounding the sacred Kaaba. Pilgrims perform Tawaf and Sa'i here as core rituals of Hajj and Umrah.

📍 Center of Makkah
Madinah

Masjid an-Nabawi (The Prophet's Mosque)

The second holiest mosque in Islam, built by the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) himself. Features the iconic Green Dome and the sacred Rawdah block.

📍 Center of Madinah
Makkah

Ghar-e-Hira (Jabal al-Nour)

The mountain cave where the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) spent periods of reflection and received his very first Quranic revelations from Angel Jibreel.

📍 4km Northeast of Haram
Makkah

Jabal al-Rahmah (Mount of Mercy)

The granite hill in the plain of Arafat where the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) delivered his historic Farewell Sermon to thousands of companions.

📍 20km Southeast of Makkah
Madinah

Masjid al-Quba

The first mosque built in Islamic history, founded by the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) on his migration from Makkah. Offers the reward of one Umrah for praying here.

📍 Southern Madinah Outskirts
Madinah

Mount Uhud Battlefield & Martyrs Cemetery

The historic battleground and final resting place of 70 noble martyrs of the early Muslim community, including the Prophet's uncle Hamza.

📍 5km North of Madinah

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required to process an Umrah visa?

You need a Pakistani passport valid for at least 6 months, a computerized CNIC/NICOP, passport-sized photographs with white backgrounds, and vaccination certificates (Meningitis/Polio).

Can packages be customized for family groups?

Yes! We specialize in custom tours. We can arrange triple/quad sharing rooms, private GMC Yukon vehicles, and wheelchair-accessible services for elderly parents.

Are flights direct or connecting?

Premium and VIP packages secure direct flights via Saudi Arabian Airlines or PIA. Economy packages may utilize connections via Gulf airlines (e.g. FlyNas, Emirates, Gulf Air) to optimize budget.
